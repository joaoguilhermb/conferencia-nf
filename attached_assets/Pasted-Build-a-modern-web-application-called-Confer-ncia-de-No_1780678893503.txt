BUSINESS CONTEXT

The Municipal Tax Book (Livro Fiscal) contains invoices issued in the municipality of Rondonópolis.

The Apollo report contains invoices from multiple cities within the state.

Therefore:

The goal is NOT to identify invoices that exist in Apollo but do not exist in the Municipal Tax Book.

Those records must be ignored.

The only objective is to verify whether every invoice from the Municipal Tax Book was correctly posted in Apollo.

The Municipal Tax Book must always be treated as the source of truth.

DATA NORMALIZATION

Before comparison:

* Remove punctuation from CNPJ.
* Ignore uppercase/lowercase differences.
* Remove extra spaces.
* Normalize dates.
* Normalize monetary values.
* Ignore formatting differences that do not affect actual values.

COMPARISON STRATEGY

The reconciliation must start from the Livro Fiscal.

For each invoice found in the Livro Fiscal:

1. Search for a matching invoice in Apollo.
2. Determine whether it exists.
3. Compare all relevant fields.
4. Identify possible posting mistakes.

Do NOT create alerts simply because an invoice exists in Apollo but does not exist in the Livro Fiscal.

Those invoices may belong to other cities and are irrelevant.

FIELDS TO COMPARE

* Número da Nota Fiscal
* Data de Emissão
* Razão Social
* CNPJ
* Valor Bruto
* Valor Líquido
* Valor do ISS

CLASSIFICATIONS

If all fields match:

Status:
"Conciliada"

If the invoice is not found:

Status:
"Nota Não Localizada"

If the invoice exists but contains different information:

Status:
"Nota Divergente"

If a likely match exists with a different invoice number:

Status:
"Possível Erro de Lançamento"

SMART MATCHING

When the invoice number is not found:

Search Apollo records using:

* Same CNPJ
* Same Razão Social
* Same Valor Bruto
* Same Valor Líquido
* Same Valor do ISS
* Similar Data de Emissão

Detect situations such as:

* Missing digits
* Extra digits
* Inverted digits
* Similar numbering
* Typing mistakes

Example:

Livro Fiscal:
Nota 40

Apollo:
Nota 4

If the remaining information matches:

Observação:
"Possível lançamento com número incorreto."

Ação Recomendada:
"Verificar lançamento e corrigir a numeração da nota fiscal."

CONFIDENCE SCORE

For possible posting errors, calculate a confidence score between 0% and 100%.

Use:

* CNPJ similarity
* Supplier similarity
* Gross amount
* Net amount
* ISS amount
* Issue date
* Invoice number similarity
